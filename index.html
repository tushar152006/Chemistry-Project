Absorption and Emission Spectrum

Introduction

Absorption and emission spectra provide insight into atomic structures by analyzing how atoms absorb and emit light at specific wavelengths. Below you will find explanations, formulas, and interactive graphs.

Formulas

Energy of Photon: E = hν = hc/λ

Wavelength (λ): λ = c/ν

Where E is energy, h is Planck's constant, ν is frequency, c is speed of light, and λ is wavelength.
